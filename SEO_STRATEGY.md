## 7. Authority Building Strategy

- **GitHub:** Maintain active commit history, write clean READMEs for pinned projects.
- **Writing:** Publish technical articles on the portfolio blog and cross-post to Medium/Dev.to.
- **Documentation:** Treat project case studies as technical documentation (Architecture, Challenges, Solutions).

---

## 8. Writing Voice Rules

- **Tone:** Confident, Technical, Clear, Outcome-driven.
- **Style:** Active voice ("I built", "I architected" vs "Was built").
- **Length:** Short, punchy sentences. Avoid walls of text.
- **Credibility:** Use specific metrics where possible (e.g., "Improved load time by 40%").
